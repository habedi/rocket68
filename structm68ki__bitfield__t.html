uint32 m68ki_bitfield_t::lo

When dealing with bitfields to/from memory, we need to support offsets not aligned to a byte boundary. Since the maximum width of a bitfield is 32, that means accessing at most 5 bytes. When writing back the result, we need to leave the bits outside the accessed bitfield alone. To do that, we track the 5 bytes we read here
